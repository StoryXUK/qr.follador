A Legacy of Excellence Since 1769

Step into a world where tradition meets innovation, where passion and dedication converge to create a legacy of exceptional Italian wines. The Follador family, synonymous with excellence in winemaking, has been crafting exceptional wines since 1769 when Giovanni Follador received the prestigious Wine Quality Award from the Doge of Venice, Alvise IV Mocenigo.

Every bottle is a testament to the enduring spirit of the Follador family.

Sustainability

Follador is dedicated to sustainable viticulture and environmental protection, meeting the expectations of an informed clientele. Determination, dedication, and commitment define our daily work, embodying our core value of preserving our territory for generations.

The Region

Nestled in the romantic Veneto region, near historic Venice, lies the ideal Prosecco "Terroir." Col San Martino, at the heart of this Prosecco haven, offers superb wine-growing conditions and Follador Vineyards flourish in this perfect blend of micro-climate, regional support, and optimal soil composition.

About Follodor

The Follador Family cares a lot about its cultural heritage in the production of wine. It has neither stood still in the past, nor is it intimidated by modern times, but has quietly married traditional cultivation techniques in the vineyards with the most modern production technologies, this to obtain wines highly regarded even by experts in the sector.
